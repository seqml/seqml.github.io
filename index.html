Sequence Machine Learning

This page lists some machine learning related research at Microsoft Research Asia, conducted by the team led by Kan Ren. The research topics cover spatial-temporal data mining, sequential decision making with reinforcement learning, meta-learning and ensemble learning, etc. Some research are open-sourced via AutoRL and SeqML.
